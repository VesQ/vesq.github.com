Platformer

A not-so-finished game made with canvas+JavaScript

Your goal is to collect all the coins (the spinning grey ones) and then come back to the start. The red thing that is supposed to represent a door will have turned green. Touch it and see something uncool happen!

You can move around with arrow keys and jump with Z.

Pause-button works as an emergency stop. You will have to refresh the page to play again.

Oh, and do try the F11, it will bring the game to fullscreen! Although it will most likely lag and get you stuck at the walls, but it's anyways a nice functionality :)
